Beef Bourguignon

The dish is often considered "traditional" but in fact is first documented in the 19th century. Other recipes called "à la Bourguignonne" with similar garnishes are found in the mid-19th century for leg of lamb and for rabbit.

Beef bourguignon is generally accompanied with boiled potatoes or noodles.
